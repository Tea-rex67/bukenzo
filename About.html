About Us

Sethage and Partners Legal Practitioners (LP)

Sethage and Partners Legal Practitioners (LP), hereinafter referred to as “the Firm,” is a full-service law firm renowned for its excellence and service delivery. The Firm boasts extensive experience across various fields of law, with a particular emphasis on litigation and solicitors’ work both within and outside Nigeria. Our expertise spans election petitions, matrimonial causes, land matters, and more. We are dedicated to providing efficient services to our diverse clientele, which includes individuals, corporate organizations, foreign companies, and governmental agencies.

Initially established in 2007 as Center Point Legal, the Firm has become synonymous with professionalism and industry in Nigeria and among leading international corporations. In addition to our strong representation in commercial law for major corporate entities across various economic sectors, we have a reputable legacy for providing comprehensive legal advisory and dispute resolution services.

We are committed to delivering value-driven, competent, and expert legal services as a reputable company. This commitment is reflected in our firm profile, which serves as a testament to our pledge of excellence and dedication to our clients’ needs.

The firm is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and has its registered Office addresses in Lagos State, with presence in Abuja the Federal Capital Territory of Nigeria. The firm boasts of quality professionals with eye for top-class legal representation of clients’ varied interests. We focus on achieving timely intervention on behalf of our clients, working all the way and in partnership with them to achieve their desired goals as we strongly believe that our clients’ success is ultimately ours.

TELL US YOUR CASE

We place a very high premium on the integrity of our services and representations with a drive for technically sound and commercially rewarding outcomes for our clients. We offer services in major sectors of the economy such as the business and banking, insurance, trade and investments, minerals and mines, telecommunications, constructions, transportations, commercial and manufacturing companies.

Our versatility covers both the traditional and the emerging areas of legal practice with quality advisory services in broad-range corporate issues such as business, finance, recovery, regulatory and other professional endeavors. We also provide reliable interface between the private and public sectors of the economy, between government and corporate concerns, and between indigenous enterprises, bodies, organizations etc.
