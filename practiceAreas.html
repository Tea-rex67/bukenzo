Litigation

Boasting extensive expertise in handling complex cases across a wide array of domains, our firm has always excelled in litigation. We are proficient in both trial and appellate practice, representing clients in federal and state courts, as well as before regulatory agencies at all levels.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

We understand the threat that litigation often poses to the continued success of businesses, partnerships and corporations; which is why we are staunch advocates of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) in deserving situations.

Patents, Trademarks & Intellectual Property

The firm acts as Trademark Agents and has established itself as experts in the protection of intellectual property rights as well as in negotiating and drafting franchise agreements.

Admirality and Shipping

Our emphasis which is on maritime litigation and arbitration covers Cargo claims, charter party and bill of lading disputes, personal injury, collision, maritime insurance. We are also involved in sale and purchase of ships, mortgages, financing, leasing and registration.

Co-operate services

Our Firm handles varieties of matters including organizations and formation of corporate entities such as limited liability companies, partnerships, dissolution, management and other corporate activities such as debt recovery, contract reviews, negotiations and sales, purchases, merger proposition and facilitation, partnership with other business entities, promotions as well as all pre incorporation and post incorporation activities.

Real Estate

We represent buyers and sellers in different types of transactions on commercial and residential projects. We thoroughly immerse ourselves in the details of the business aspect of each deal. Our services extend to contract negotiation, preparing purchase agreements, deeds and bills of sale, perfection of title documents, registration of charges, landlord/tenant or lessor/lessee disputes, commercial and retail leases, construction agreements, obtaining building permits and approvals from relevant regulatory agencies.

Private Client

The firm attaches considerable importance to private clients and offers quality advise on tax and financial planning, contracts, damages of all ramifications, settlements of wills, matrimonial causes etc. In any of the options above, we provide the best legal services with poise professionalism and dedication.

Oil and Gas

We review contracts and advise clients on oil fields/wells for oil companies especially open to representing communities in the South-South region and other oil producing regions of Nigeria over environmental hazards done to their communities as well as advise companies over disputes with regards to returns on oil exploration.
